the freshest music player out there

Organized

FRESHMusicPlayer is designed to enable you to quickly and easily navigate your music library, no matter how large it is. With Auto Import, FRESHMusicPlayer can also automatically import any additions into any of your music folders.

Full-Featured

FRESHMusicPlayer supports all of the modern playback features you expect, including playlists, EQ, ReplayGain loudness normalization, and lyrics. There's also a mini player and a fullscreen party/focus mode.

Integrated

FRESHMusicPlayer integrates with the System Transport Controls, supports last.fm scrobbling, and optionally, Discord Rich Presence. In addition, you can pull the correct metadata for your tracks from music databases like Discogs and MusicBrainz.

Other Features

  • - Tag editor
  • - Auto queue
  • - Playback plugin support
