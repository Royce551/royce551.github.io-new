the freshest music player out there

Organized

FRESHMusicPlayer automatically organizes your music library into artists and albums. With Auto Import, FRESHMusicPlayer can automatically import any additions to your music folder. You can also find that track you want to play right away with search, create playlists, and correct any tagging mistakes using the built in tag editor.

Powerful

FRESHMusicPlayer includes a ton of features, including an equalizer, a lyrics viewer, mini player, and fullscreen view. There's support for most popular media formats out of the box, with optional support for CDs and YouTube videos using plugins. FRESHMusicPlayer also deeply integrates with the system and services like Discord, last.fm, Discogs, and MusicBrainz.
